Keeping My Roots Alive

During Diwali, I decorated my dorm window with marigold lights, imagining family gatherings back home. A roommate asked, “What’s Diwali?” and I described fireworks painting the night sky with hope.

Finding Community

At the South Asian Student Association welcome, a senior greeted me warmly: “You’re one of us now.” We danced to Bollywood beats and I realized belonging transcends distance.

Personal Traditions

Every Sunday, I make masala chai and invite friends: “Join me for chai?” Conversations flow like the tea—warm, comforting, and bridging our worlds in each shared sip.
