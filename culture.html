Classroom Culture Shock

In the U.S., students debate openly; back home, we respect silence. On day one, my heart raced as the professor asked, “Any questions?” I raised my hand, voice trembling, and discovered strength in curiosity.

First Snow Day ❄️

I ran outside in flip-flops—rookie mistake—but the snow was magical. A friend called out, “Here, borrow my boots!” We built a snowman and laughter echoed across campus green.

Dining Hall Tales

Standing in the lunch line, a student offered, “Spicy curry? It’s from India!” I smiled proudly and shared stories of my hometown kitchen as we swapped spice secrets over steaming rice.
