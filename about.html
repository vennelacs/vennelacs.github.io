How It All Began

Born and raised in India, I grew up celebrating vibrant festivals, sharing meals with family, and dreaming big. Small alleys echoed with laughter during Holi, and my grandmother’s stories painted each morning with hope.

Leaving Home

That suitcase carousel felt like the point of no return. Clutching my passport, I whispered to myself: “I can do this.” A farewell hug from Mom lingered in my mind as I stepped into unknown skies.

First Conversation

On my first campus dinner, a friendly face said, “Try the cornbread—it’s our staple!” Hesitant, I took a bite and smiled, realizing that every shared meal bridged worlds and hearts.

Roommate Reunion

Late at night, my roommate blinked sleepily: “Night owl, huh?” We laughed, swapping stories of late-night study marathons and morning chai rituals, forging a bond beyond borders.
